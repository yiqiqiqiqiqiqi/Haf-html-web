Malaysia Independence

The Journey to Malaysia's Independence

Early Struggles

The people of Malaysia launched several resistance movements during the colonial period, laying the foundation for independence.

Formation of the Alliance

Various ethnic groups united to form the Federation of Malaya, providing a strong organizational framework for the independence movement.

Independence Day

On August 31, 1957, Malaysia officially declared independence, becoming a sovereign nation.
